# Contributor Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to follow the community standards described below.

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in the Flex Audit project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate. 
- Be collaborative and assume good intentions.
- Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers. Contributors who do not follow these guidelines may be asked to stop or be excluded from the project.

*This Code of Conduct is adapted from the contributor covenant.*
